Mohinga

Ingredients

  • Gram flour and/or crushed toasted rice
  • Garlic
  • Shallots or onions
  • Lemongrass
  • Ginger
  • Fish paste
  • Fish sauce
  • Catfish (or other types of fish such as Mrigal carp)

Steps

  1. Preparing Ingredients:
    • Crush toasted rice or prepare gram flour.
    • Mince garlic.
    • Slice shallots or onions.
    • Chop lemongrass and ginger.
    • Prepare fish paste and fish sauce.
    • Clean and cut catfish or chosen fish into pieces.
  2. Cook Fish:
    • Boil catfish pieces in water until cooked.
    • Remove fish, debone, and flake the meat. Set aside the broth.
  3. Make Broth Base:
    • In a large pot, heat oil.
    • Add minced garlic, sliced shallots or onions, chopped lemongrass, and ginger.
    • Saut√© until fragrant.
